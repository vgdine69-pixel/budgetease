Can I export my expense data?

Yes! Click the "Download" button on any page to export your data in multiple formats including Excel (.xlsx), PDF (.pdf), CSV (.csv), and JSON (.json).

How do I change my currency?

You can change your preferred currency from the currency selector dropdown at the top of the Dashboard page. We support multiple currencies including INR, USD, EUR, GBP, and more.

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Your financial information is stored securely and is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
